# Cyber Attacks Research - Notes

This file summarises major cyber attacks relevant to the assignment.

---

## WannaCry (2017)

- Ransomware attack.
- Exploited SMBv1 vulnerability.
- Impacted NHS and organisations worldwide.

---

## Equifax Breach (2017)

- Apache Struts vulnerability.
- 147 million records exposed.
- Over $1.4 billion in damages.

---

## Facebook–Cambridge Analytica (2018)

- Misuse of personal data.
- Major reputational damage.

---

## Key Lessons

- Patch systems regularly.
- Use strong access controls.
- Monitor network traffic.
- Train staff on cyber hygiene.
